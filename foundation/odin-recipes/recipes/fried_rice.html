Fried Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • Rice 1 cup
  • Oil 1 teaspoon
  • Bell pepper 100 gm
  • Peas
  • onion

Steps:

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly with clean water
  2. Boil the rice in cooker and take one whistle
  3. Dry the rice and cool it down for 30 minutes
  4. Cover the rise in a bowl and put it in a freezer
  5. Take the rice out after 3-4 hours
  6. Peel and Chop the onions in small pieces
  7. Chop other vegetabels as well e.g. Bell pepper, carrot etc.
  8. Heat the pan and put some oil in it
  9. Put some cumin seed followed by chopped onions. Keep heating until they turn brown
  10. Put coppted bell pepper
  11. Finally, put the rice and stir it for a while
  12. Your food is ready now.
