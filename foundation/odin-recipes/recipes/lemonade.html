Lemonade

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 Lemons medium
  • 1 glass water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Steps:

  1. Fill the glass with water
  2. Squeeze two lemons in it. Make sure to filter the seeds out with the help of squeezer.
  3. Put salt and sugar in it
  4. Stir with a spoon
  5. (Optional) Garnish with few mint leaves
